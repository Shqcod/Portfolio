Did you know that spending time with loved ones can actually make you feel like time moves faster? This phenomenon, called "time compression," happens because your brain processes enjoyable moments more efficiently, making them seem shorter in hindsight. On the other hand, boring or stressful situations feel longer due to "time dilation," where your brain pays more attention to each passing second. So, if time flies when you're having fun—it’s not just a saying, it's science!
